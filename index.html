Bio

I have been working as a Research Associate in Low-Cost Automation Systems in the Distributed Information and Automation Laboratory (DIAL) at the Institute for Manufacturing (IfM), the University of Cambridge since July 2020. Before my current position, I worked as an Assistant Professor in the Computer Engineering Department at Pamukkale University, Turkey, for two years. I received my PhD in Information Systems from Middle East Technical University (METU), Turkey, and my PhD dissertation was awarded the “Best Thesis in Information Systems” in 2018 by the Parlar Education and Research Foundation, one of the prestigious scientific bodies in Turkey.
